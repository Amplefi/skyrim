Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install mods manually? ›

  1. Download a plugin (.esp) and the associated resources archive (.bsa). Note: Not all mods have or need archives.
  2. Copy the plugin and archive to your Steam\steamapps\common\Skyrim\Data folder.
  3. Enable the plugin with your choice of mod manager.

Can I port your mods to consoles? Or upload your mods anywhere? ›

  • No, you may not port my mods to consoles.
  • No, you may not upload my mods elsewhere.
  • No, you may not include my mods in any compilation.
  • No, you may not alter my mods and pass them off as your own.
